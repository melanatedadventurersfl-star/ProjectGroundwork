# Founder Handbook

> A living source of truth for how the company thinks, decides, designs, and grows community-driven products.

## Purpose

The Founder Handbook captures the philosophy, methods, product standards, and shared architecture that guide every brand in the portfolio.

## Core idea

We are a connection company that builds communities through intentionally curated experiences.

- **Purpose:** Connect communities.
- **Method:** Curate experiences.
- **Outcome:** Belonging.

## Working rules

- This handbook is versioned and expected to evolve.
- Major product, brand, and architecture decisions should be documented.
- Brands remain distinct even when they share platform capabilities.
- Technology serves real-world experiences rather than replacing them.
- Every roadmap begins with the barriers facing a specific community.
