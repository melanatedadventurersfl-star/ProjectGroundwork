# JaxBlack Blueprint

## Identity

**Category:** Black-centered local discovery and connection community  
**Stage:** Active community concept and product definition

## Purpose

Strengthen Black connection in Jacksonville by helping people discover events, businesses, opportunities, relationships, and ways to participate locally.

## Primary community

Black residents, professionals, creatives, entrepreneurs, newcomers, organizations, and community builders in the Jacksonville area.

## Core barriers

- Fragmented information about local events and opportunities
- Difficulty finding culturally relevant spaces and businesses
- Networking experiences that feel transactional or cliquish
- New residents not knowing where to begin
- Local organizations repeatedly reaching the same limited audiences
- Weak continuity after one-time events

## Core experiences

- Curated local event and opportunity discovery
- Welcoming social and professional gatherings
- Business and organization discovery
- Interest-based circles and introductions
- Community calendars, guides, and recurring traditions
- Pathways from attendee to contributor, host, mentor, and ambassador

## Community journey

Observer → Attendee → Familiar Face → Member → Connector → Host or Mentor → Ambassador

## Product DNA

We celebrate hospitality, collaboration, local pride, cultural authenticity, mutual support, and opening doors for others.

We protect trust, useful curation, newcomer inclusion, and the distinction between meaningful networking and shallow contact collection.

## Boundaries

JaxBlack is geographically and culturally focused. It should not become a generic city directory or absorb the outdoor mission of Melanated Adventurers. Shared tools may support both, but their communities and public identities remain distinct.

## Initial success indicators

- Newcomers attending and returning
- Connections continuing after events
- Member referrals
- Local businesses and organizations receiving meaningful engagement
- Participants becoming hosts, mentors, or connectors
- Useful event and opportunity discovery
- Increased collaboration across existing local groups
