# The Compass

## Identity

We are a connection company that builds communities through intentionally curated experiences.

## Purpose

Connect communities.

## Mission

Create products, services, and experiences that help people discover shared passions, form meaningful relationships, and find places where they belong.

## North Star

> Connection is our product.

Camping, craft beer, outdoor adventure, local networking, events, and technology are different mediums. The intended outcome is stronger human connection.

## Company promise

We design curated experiences that remove barriers, strengthen communities, and create opportunities for people to belong.

## How we connect

We connect:

1. People to people.
2. People to experiences.
3. People to places.
4. People to knowledge.
5. People to opportunities.

## Golden rules

- Technology should lead people into the real world, not replace it.
- Every decision should strengthen a community.
- Experience comes before software.
- Brands have identities; platforms have capabilities.
- Build depth before breadth.
- Connection should continue after the app closes.

## Decision question

> What problem are we solving, and for whom?
