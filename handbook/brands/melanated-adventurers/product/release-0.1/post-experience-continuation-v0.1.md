# Post-Experience Continuation Path v0.1

Status: Draft for review
Linear: PRO-15

## Purpose

Preserve connection after departure and give each participant a clear, low-pressure next step within 48 hours.

## Member outcome

> The experience did not disappear when I left. I know how to reconnect and what I can do next.

## Follow-up window

The primary follow-up is sent within 24 hours and no later than 48 hours after the experience ends.

## Required follow-up

1. **Thank and recognize**
   - Thank participants and acknowledge hosts, volunteers, and shared moments.
2. **Reflect**
   - Ask no more than three lightweight questions:
     - Did you feel welcomed?
     - Did you make or strengthen a meaningful connection?
     - What would help you participate again?
3. **Reconnect**
   - Point to the appropriate existing group or channel.
   - Provide a privacy-respecting method for reconnecting with people met at the experience.
4. **Continue**
   - Recommend one or two relevant upcoming experiences, not an endless calendar.
5. **Contribute**
   - Offer optional contribution paths such as helping at check-in, sharing a story, assisting with gear, or joining a planning conversation.
6. **Close exceptions**
   - Follow up personally where a newcomer reported confusion, isolation, accessibility issues, or a poor welcome.

## Progression ladder

Attendee -> Returning participant -> Helper -> Contributor -> Experience lead -> Community leader

The ladder is descriptive, not compulsory. People may participate repeatedly without pursuing leadership.

## Connection rules

- Do not publish attendee contact information.
- Do not create automatic connections without consent.
- Prefer facilitated introductions or opt-in group channels.
- Avoid requiring a public social feed.

## Shared and MA-specific ownership

Groundwork owns follow-up triggers, reflection capture, next-step recommendations, consent, and progression records. MA owns message tone, community channels, contribution options, recognition, and personal recovery conversations.

## Success measures

- Follow-up delivered within 48 hours
- Reflection response rate
- Reported meaningful connection
- Intent to return
- Registration for another experience
- Actual return within 90 days
- Opt-in movement into helper or contributor roles
