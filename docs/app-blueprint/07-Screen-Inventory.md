# 07. Screen Inventory

## Document Status

- **Version:** 1.0
- **Milestone:** 1 — Product Foundation
- **Status:** Approved working inventory

## Purpose

This document identifies the screens required to support the member journey and separates launch needs from later phases. It is not a final sitemap or wireframe specification. Each screen will receive detailed states, components, and flows during Milestone 2.

## Priority Definitions

- **P0:** Required for the first complete usable release.
- **P1:** High-value enhancement after the core journey works.
- **P2:** Future expansion.
- **Role:** Intended primarily for hosts, volunteers, chapter leaders, or administrators.

## Launch Screen Set

The smallest complete launch should include:

1. account creation and sign-in;
2. onboarding;
3. Trailhead;
4. Explore and search;
5. Adventure Detail;
6. registration, payment, and confirmation;
7. preparation hub;
8. check-in;
9. Journey;
10. Passport identity and stamps;
11. Community basics;
12. Campfire activity;
13. account, privacy, support, and safety;
14. post-adventure feedback and memory capture.

## Current Decisions

- The launch must support a full Adventure lifecycle rather than disconnected browsing screens.
- Direct messaging is excluded.
- Host and chapter tools are documented but separated from the first member release.
- Every P0 screen must include accessibility, loading, empty, error, and low-connectivity consideration during Milestone 2.

## Open Questions
