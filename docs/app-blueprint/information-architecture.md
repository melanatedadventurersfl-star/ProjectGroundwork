# Melanated Adventurers Information Architecture

## Purpose

The app is organized around real-world experiences, not feature accumulation. Trailhead acts as the adaptive connective layer across the ecosystem.

## Primary navigation

### Trailhead
Adaptive dashboard that changes according to the member's Adventure Queue, readiness, lifecycle state, and urgent updates.

### Explore
Find the next experience through featured adventures, calendar, map, categories, search, nearby experiences, saved adventures, and bucket-list items.

### Community
Intentional people-centered interaction, including chapters, friends, groups, discussions, ride shares, gear exchange, volunteer opportunities, and event-related connections.

### Passport
Progress and accomplishment, including stamps, badges, milestones, places visited, activities completed, challenges, volunteer service, and Adventure DNA.

### Menu
Access to the wider ecosystem without overcrowding the primary navigation.

## Menu structure

### My Adventures
- Primary Adventure
- Current Adventure
- Upcoming Adventures
- Waitlists
- Draft or incomplete registrations
- Recently completed adventures
- Archived adventures

### Journey
Chronological record of experiences, memories, photos, reflections, people met, and places visited.

### Campfire
Cross-app activity center for host announcements, event updates, new comments, photos, community activity, weather changes, and Passport milestones.

Campfire is distinct from notifications. Notifications are reserved for items requiring attention.

### Build-A-Camp
Bookings, packages, rentals, reservations, equipment, and setup services.

### MANA
Education, stewardship, conservation, youth programs, service, and volunteer opportunities.

### Profile
Identity, preferences, interests, Adventure DNA, accessibility needs, emergency contacts, transportation, camping experience, and gear owned.

### Settings
Account, privacy, notification preferences, security, appearance, and support.

## Global utilities

### Search
Search across adventures, people, locations, chapters, groups, hosts, photos, guides, challenges, and help content.

### Notifications
Actionable alerts only, such as payment reminders, waiver deadlines, schedule changes, safety notices, and check-in requirements.

### Campfire access
Campfire should remain reachable globally, but it should not be represented by an unexplained flame icon. The control should communicate activity or inbox behavior clearly.

## Core destinations

### Adventure Detail
The command center for a single adventure. Its sections may include:

- Overview
- Registration
- Readiness
- Attendees and groups
- Schedule
- Packing
- Meals
- Weather
- Transportation
- Maps and directions
- Campfire updates
- Photos
- Safety and emergency information
- FAQs
- Reflection and memories

Section priority changes across the adventure lifecycle.

### Adventure Central
A complete view of the member's Adventure Queue, including readiness, next actions, status, and completed experiences.

## Navigation principle

Primary navigation should stay stable while Trailhead content adapts. Adaptive behavior belongs in the dashboard and adventure surfaces, not in constantly moving the main navigation.

## Product boundary

A feature belongs when it makes a real-world adventure easier, safer, richer, more connected, or more memorable. Features designed mainly to increase screen time should be excluded.
